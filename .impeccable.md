## Design Context

### Users
Administrative staff and owners of an "Oficina Mecânica" (Mechanic Shop). They use the app to manage customer data, vehicles, service orders (OS), and inventory. The context is often busy, requiring quick access to key metrics and fast interaction patterns to finalize services and process payments.

### Brand Personality
**Robust, Clean, Executive.**
The interface should evoke confidence and professionalism. It feels like a high-end tool ("Noir Premium") rather than a generic utility.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Refined Professional Minimalism.**
- **Theme**: Light mode with strong black/dark-slate accents.
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif (currently Inter).
- **Colors**: High contrast. Pure black (#000) is currently used for primary, but could be softened slightly for a more premium feel. Success (Emerald) and Error (Red) used for status.
- **References**: High-end automotive dashboards, executive financial apps.

### Design Principles
1. **Data over Decoration**: Every visual element should serve to clarify information or facilitate an action.
2. **Rhythmic Spacing**: Use space to group related information (e.g., service details) and separate distinct sections (e.g., summary stats vs. activity logs).
3. **Intentional Hierarchy**: Use weight and space to guide the user's eye to the most critical information first (e.g., overdue service orders or low stock).
4. **Fast Interaction**: Minimize clicks for common tasks like finalizing a service order.
5. **Modern Precision**: Sharp borders, subtle shadows, and consistent border radii (12px) to maintain a cohesive, high-quality feel.
